OSCAR Docs

Open Source Copter for Academic Research (OSCAR) is a student-made, open-source drone platform designed for academic research and education. This documentation provides comprehensive guides, tutorials, and reference materials to help you get started with OSCAR and make the most of its capabilities.

About Us

Made by students,
for students.

Whether you are a student, researcher, educator, or hobbyist, OSCAR is designed to be accessible and adaptable for a wide range of applications, with open source documentation and resources to support your projects and learning journey.
